Meet P1Harmony

A 6-member South Korean boy band of FNC entertainment, consisting of Keeho, Theo, Jiung, Intak, Soul, and Jongseob. They were introduced through the movie P1H: The Beginning of a New World in August 2020 and officially debuted on October 28, 2020 with the EP Disharmony: Stand Out.
